## SCHEDULING SETUP (do this first)

### Step 1 — Meta Business Suite (Instagram + Facebook)
1. Go to business.facebook.com
2. Select your DraftMeet page
3. Click "Create post" → choose Instagram + Facebook simultaneously
4. Paste the copy from the platform files
5. Upload the image (generated with Imagen 3)
6. Click "Schedule" → set the date/time below

### Step 2 — Buffer (LinkedIn + X/Twitter)
1. Go to buffer.com → connect LinkedIn company page + X account
2. Click "+ New Post" → select the platform
3. Paste copy, attach image, set schedule time
4. Click "Add to Queue"

### Step 3 — Instagram Reels (manual or scheduled via Meta)
- Reels must be uploaded from the Instagram app or Meta Business Suite
- Generate Reel videos with Veo3, edit in CapCut (add captions, music), then schedule
