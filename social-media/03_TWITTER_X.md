## X PERFORMANCE NOTES

- **Best tweet times:** Mon–Thu 8–10 AM, 12 PM, 5–7 PM. Weekends are lighter but still worth 1 post/day.
- **Thread strategy:** Post each tweet in the thread 30 seconds apart using "Reply to your own tweet." Do NOT post them all at once — let each one land before the next.
- **Early engagement matters most.** The first 30 minutes after posting = the algorithm test. Engage actively in that window.
- **Hashtags:** Max 2 per tweet. Use specific ones: `#scheduling`, `#freelancers`, `#founders`. Avoid generic `#marketing`.
- **Retweet your own threads:** After 6–12 hours, quote-tweet your thread with "in case you missed this:" — it refreshes reach.
