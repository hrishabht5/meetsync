## INSTAGRAM PERFORMANCE NOTES

- **Reels get 2–3x more reach than static posts** right now. Prioritize them.
- **Post Reels between 11 AM–1 PM or 7–9 PM** for highest reach.
- **Use ALL caption characters.** Instagram doesn't penalize long captions.
- **Stories:** post 2–5 stories per day max. More than that = drop-off.
- **Reply to every comment** within the first hour of posting — this signals the algorithm that your content creates conversations.
- **Hashtag strategy:** Mix of 3 large (#productivity — 50M+ posts), 5 medium (#schedulingtool — 500K posts), 3 niche (#DraftMeet, #onlinebooking — smaller but highly relevant).
- **Save rate matters.** Posts with tips/instructions get saved — saved posts are heavily weighted in Instagram's algorithm. Posts I3 (tips carousel) should perform best.
